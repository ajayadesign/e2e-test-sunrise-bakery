Our Story Begins in Portland

Sunrise Bakery started as a small family venture in the heart of Portland. Inspired by the city's vibrant food culture, we set out to create pastries that bring people together.

Today, we remain committed to our roots: sourcing local, organic ingredients and crafting every pastry with care. It's not just baking—it's our way of sharing joy.

What We Stand For

Fresh Ingredients

We partner with local farmers to bring you the freshest, organic ingredients in every bite.

Sustainability

From eco-friendly packaging to reducing food waste, we're committed to protecting our planet.

The Art of Baking

Every pastry is a work of art, crafted with precision and love to bring you the perfect treat.
