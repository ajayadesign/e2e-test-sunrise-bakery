Our Story

Sunrise Bakery was born from a love of baking and a passion for bringing people together. What started as a small family project has grown into a cornerstone of our community. Every loaf, pastry, and treat we create is made with heart and dedication to time-honored artisan techniques.

We believe in the power of good food to connect people, and we’re committed to using locally sourced ingredients to craft flavors that feel like home. At Sunrise Bakery, you’re not just a customer—you’re family.

Sustainability at Sunrise Bakery

At Sunrise Bakery, we believe that great taste begins with great responsibility. That’s why we partner with local farmers and producers to source the freshest, eco-friendly ingredients for our baked goods.

From reducing food waste in our kitchens to using compostable packaging, we’re committed to protecting the planet while delighting your taste buds.

Together, we can enjoy delicious treats while making a positive impact on our community and the environment.
